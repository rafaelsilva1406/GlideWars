# Glide Wars - Gameplay Guide

Welcome to **Glide Wars**! This guide will help you master the skies and conquer all seven continents.

## ðŸŽ® Getting Started

### Your First Flight
1. **Launch the game** and press any key at the splash screen
2. From the **Main Menu**, select "START"
3. Choose the **Tutorial** continent (highly recommended for first-time players)
4. Read the **Tutorial Instructions** screen carefully
5. Press **SPACE** to begin your flight!

## âŒ¨ï¸ Controls

### Basic Movement
- **W / â†‘**: Climb upward (counter gravity)
- **S / â†“**: Dive downward (accelerate fall)
- **A / â†**: Move left
- **D / â†’**: Move right

The glider **always moves forward automatically** - your job is to navigate up, down, left, and right!

### Advanced Controls
- **SHIFT or TAB**: **Speed Boost**
  - Provides 1.8x speed (80% increase)
  - Drains boost energy: 50/second
  - Recharges when not boosting: 20/second
  - Use strategically in tight situations!

- **SPACE**: Fire weapon (when equipped)
- **ESC**: Pause game / Return to menu

### HUD Display
**Top Left Panel:**
- **HEALTH**: Your current health (100 max)
- **BOOST**: Speed boost energy meter
- **SCORE**: Current score
- **HIGH**: Best score for this continent
- **WEAPON**: Current weapon name
- **AMMO**: Remaining ammunition
- **DRONE**: Companion timer (when active)

**Top Center:**
- Continent name

**Top Right Panel:**
- **TIME**: Remaining time to complete level
- **CHECKPOINT**: Current checkpoint number

## ðŸŽ¯ Objectives

### Primary Goals
1. **Survive for 4-5 minutes** (varies by continent)
2. **Collect powerups** to gain weapons and health
3. **Reach checkpoints** to save your progress
4. **Defeat the boss** that spawns at ~3:30-4:30
5. **Complete the level** to unlock the next continent

### Bonus Objectives
- **Fly through cyan rings**: +100 points each
- **Destroy enemies**: +25 points per enemy (with drone companion)
- **Collect powerups**: 50-300 points depending on type

## ðŸ’Ž Powerups

### Health Powerups (Green)
- **Small Health**: Restores 25 HP, +50 points
- **Large Health**: Restores 50 HP, +100 points

### Weapon Powerups
- **Laser** (Red): Fast firing, 50 rounds, +200 points
- **Missile** (Yellow): Powerful, slow, 20 rounds, +250 points
- **Spread** (Purple): Three-way shot, 30 rounds, +200 points
- **Ammo Refill** (Orange): +25 ammo for current weapon, +75 points

### Special Powerups
- **Drone Companion** (Bright Green):
  - Spawns AI sidekick for 30 seconds
  - Auto-shoots enemies
  - Two behavior modes: Follow Player / Clear Ahead
  - Invulnerable to all damage
  - +300 points
  - **35% spawn rate** (most common special powerup)

### Powerup Collection
- Powerups **gravitate toward you** within 7 units
- Collection radius: 2.0 units
- Visual glow intensifies when being pulled
- Spawns every 5 seconds ahead of you

## ðŸŽ¯ Flying Rings

**Cyan glowing rings** appear randomly ahead:
- Fly through them for **+100 points**
- **Forgiving collection**: Edge detection with 5-unit radius
- Rings slowly rotate for visual effect
- Spawn every ~5 seconds
- Pure skill-based scoring - no penalties for missing

## ðŸš Drone Companion

The **green glowing powerup** is your best friend:

### Behavior Modes (switches every 5 seconds)
1. **Follow Player Mode**
   - Flies beside you with gentle wobble
   - Provides covering fire

2. **Clear Ahead Mode**
   - Flies 15 units ahead of you
   - Clears enemies from your path
   - Moves side-to-side to cover more area

### Combat
- Auto-shoots every 0.5 seconds
- Deals 20 damage per hit to enemies
- You earn +25 points for each enemy hit
- Green projectiles (distinguishable from yours)

### Duration
- Active for **30 seconds** after pickup
- Timer shown in HUD: "DRONE: Xs"
- Color-coded: Green (>15s), Yellow (5-15s), Orange (<5s)
- **Deactivates on death/respawn** - collect a new one!

## ðŸ”ï¸ Hazards & Enemies

### Obstacles
- **Mountains**: Large terrain features
- **Boulders**: Smaller rocks

- **Seeker**: Tracks your position

- **Spawn time**: 3:30 (Tutorial) or 4:30 (other continents)
- **3 Attack Phases**: Based on health (100%, 66%, 33%)
- **Attack Patterns**:
  - Projectile Barrage: Multiple shots toward you
  - Circular Shot: 360Â° spread attack
  - Charge Attack: Rushes forward
- **Boss stays in front**: Matches your speed (including boost!)
- **Health bar**: Displayed at top of screen
- **Bonus**: +5000 points for defeating boss

## ðŸ Checkpoints

### How They Work
- **Auto-save** at intervals (Tutorial: every 300 units, others: every 50-75 units)
- Saves your position, health, weapon, ammo, and score
- **Safe respawn**: Clears 50-unit radius of obstacles/enemies
- **3-second spawn protection**: No new enemies/obstacles spawn

### Death & Respawn
1. When you die, screen shows countdown: "RESPAWN IN: 60s"
2. Wait for countdown OR press button to restart immediately
3. Respawn at last checkpoint with saved stats
4. Drone companion **deactivates** on death

## ðŸŒ Tutorial Walkthrough

### What to Expect
- **Duration**: 4 minutes
- **7 Checkpoints**: Progress markers every 300 units
- **Boss**: Training Drone at 3:30 mark
- **Difficulty**: Easy, forgiving for learning

### Tutorial Tips
1. **First Minute**: Practice basic movement
   - Get comfortable with WASD/arrows
   - Try climbing (W) and diving (S)
   - Notice the **air trail effects** when moving vertically

2. **Second Minute**: Learn powerups
   - Collect green (health) and colored (weapons) powerups
   - Notice **magnetic pull** when you get close
   - Try flying through cyan **rings** for bonus points

3. **Third Minute**: Practice combat
   - Use weapons (SPACE) to destroy enemies
   - Try **speed boost** (SHIFT/TAB) to dodge
   - Watch your boost energy meter
   - If you find a **drone powerup** (bright green), grab it!

4. **Boss Fight (3:30-4:00)**:
   - Training Drone appears ahead
   - Dodge red/yellow projectiles
   - Shoot when safe (SPACE)
   - Use boost to evade attacks
   - Boss stays in front - you won't fly past it!

## ðŸ’¡ Pro Tips

### Energy Management
1. **Boost Strategically**: Don't waste boost flying straight
2. **Recharge Often**: Let boost recharge between obstacles
3. **Emergency Only**: Save boost for tight spots and boss fights

### Scoring Strategy
1. **Never miss rings**: Easy +100 points each
2. **Collect all powerups**: Even if you don't need them
3. **Use drone companion**: Let it farm enemy points
4. **Stay alive**: Death resets to checkpoint (may lose score progress)

### Combat Tips
1. **Prioritize survival** over shooting enemies
2. **Use terrain**: Obstacles block enemy projectiles too
3. **Drone companion** is the most valuable powerup
4. **Save ammo**: Don't waste shots, only shoot when enemies are close

### Boss Strategy
1. **Keep moving**: Don't stay in one spot
2. **Watch attack patterns**: Bosses telegraph attacks
3. **Use boost**: Essential for dodging phase 3 attacks
4. **Aim carefully**: Boss has a lot of health
5. **Be patient**: You have until timer runs out

## ðŸŽ¨ Art Style Guidelines

### Visual Theme
- **Era**: Retro 80s arcade aesthetic
- **Style**: Vector graphics inspired, neon colors
- **Geometry**: Angular, low-poly, wireframe-friendly
- **Colors**: High contrast - cyan, magenta, orange, green

### Technical Requirements
- **3D Models**: 100-2000 polygons (optimize for performance)
- **Textures**: Power-of-2 resolutions (64x64, 128x128, 256x256, etc.)
- **File Formats**:
  - Models: `.obj`, `.glb`, or `.fbx`
  - Textures: `.png` with alpha channel
- **Coordinate System**: Forward +Z, Up +Y, Right +X
- **Scale**: 1 unit = 1 meter

### Performance Optimization
- Keep polygon counts low for mobile/web support
- Use texture atlases where possible
- Compress PNG files without quality loss
- Optimize particle counts for 60 FPS gameplay
